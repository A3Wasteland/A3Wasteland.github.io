Donations

By making a voluntary donation to A3Wasteland, you reward the developers for their hard work and time invested in making an open-source Wasteland gamemode freely available to all. Donating will not give you any in-game perk or advantage over other players, in accordance with Bohemia's Game Content Usage Rules. Also, donating will not give you any priority in the development process, e.g. requests like "I gave $X now please add Y feature" will be declined.

Most of the money received will be spent toward college tuition and website fees.
